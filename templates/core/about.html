The Piece

Carnival of the Animals

The Carnival of the Animals is a humorous musical suite of fourteen movements by the French Romantic composer Camille Saint-Saëns. The Carnival of the Animals is supposed to be fun. Each of the suite's 14 movements introduces us to a different animal or group of animals, with a small number of instruments mimicking their voices or the way they move. Starting with the lion's roar and slowing to reflect the elephant's bulk, Saint-Saens pokes fun at the music of his time. The piece has become one of Saint-Saëns's best-known works.

Face Painting

The festival will feature face-painting, allowing your child to express their creativity in a fun and delightful manner! All materials are 100% safe and we will have trained volunteers on-hand for assistance.

Simply defined, face painting is the highest degree with which one can express oneself by painting on their face.

Cupcake Walk

A chance to win a cupcake! The game begins when the music starts; children walk around in a circle as the music is playing. When the music stops, the children stop. A volunteer will pull out a number from a hat and the child standing at the number wins a cupcake!
